<h1 style="color: black; font-size: 40px;" data-darkreader-inline-color="">Minecraft Gamma</h1>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Minecraft Gamma is an overhaul mod for Release 1.2.5. It's meant to be a culmination of all great aspects of many versions of Minecraft, with a big focus on Beta. Think of it like a hybrid. Then there's some weird stuff. Feature list below.&nbsp;</span></p>
<h1 style="color: black; font-size: 40px;" data-darkreader-inline-color=""><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Charmareian/MinecraftGamma/main/floodedglacier.png?token=GHSAT0AAAAAACKASXFF6YPFSK4BA5TUGCIQZMOBZ5A" alt="" width="480" height="270" /></h1>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">[Game Modes]</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">(Overall)</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-XP and Enchanting has been removed</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Food and drinks have variable consumption times </span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">(Survival)</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Hunger has been removed</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Food directly heals the player after the eating animation is complete</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">(Hardcore)</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Hunger returns in addition to Thirst&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Mobs no longer burn in the sun and Spiders are always aggressive</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">(Creative)</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Hotbar is now empty by default&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Flying speed has been increased </span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">[World Types]</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Skylands, port of the Beta 1.6 unused Sky dimension</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Alpha, port of Alpha 1.1.2_01 terrain </span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">[Biome Improvements]</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Removed Oceans completely(May return in an ocean update at a much smaller scale)</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Rivers are now slightly bigger and cut off less often&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Added Beta Beaches</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Clay now generates on the surface again</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-All biomes have a more Beta-like depth and scale</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Added Biome Variants, these generate inside of their main biome</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Deserts have been removed as a main biome</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Added the Sparse Jungle, a Jungle variant with much more livable terrain</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Added the Stony Hills, a rocky variant of the Extreme Hills</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Added the Extreme Forest, a forest variant of the Extreme Hills&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Extreme Hills now generate snow above y-108</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Remove the ugly grass, foliage and water color of Swamplands. They&rsquo;re now colored like Beta 1.7/1.8 Swamplands</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Swamplands now generate Huge Mushrooms</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Plains now generate with sparse large oak trees</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Ice Plains, now renamed to Tundras, generate sparse spruce trees</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-The Nether now generates natural Nether Wart on Soul Sand</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Structures are now off by default upon creating a world. Nether Fortresses and Desert Wells have been added to this setting</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">[Biome Additions]</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Added the Beta Savanna biome. They now have Deserts as a biome variant</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Added the unused Beta Ice Desert as a main biome. They have snowy Shrublands(cold Savanna) as a biome variant&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Added the Beta Rainforest</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Added the Flooded Forest, based off of Infdev 415 terrain. Very watery, very hilly, with dense large oak trees throughout</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">[Block Additions]</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Pillow, crafted with 9 Feathers. Breaks instantly with your fist and protects you from all fall damage</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Smooth Stone, using the unused full block Stone Slab texture. Smelt Stone in a Furnace</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Added storage blocks for Redstone, Coal and Charcoal</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Bellflower, a flower for colder biomes. Crafts into Purple Dye</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Cobwebs can be crafted from 9 String and vice versa</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Added Wool Carpets</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Added proper Wooden Slabs for all wood types. Petrified Oak Slabs can be created by smelting Wooden Slabs in the Furnace</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Added Mossy Stairs and Slabs</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Mossy Cobblestone can now be crafted from one Vine and one Cobblestone</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Sponges are now functional</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Leaves now only have a 1 in 4 chance of damaging Tools</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-TNT can now be crafted with Paper instead of Sand</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Glass now drops when mined and can be mine faster</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Rightclicking Cobblestone with a pickaxe turns it into Gravel, making Gravel renewable</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-4 Coarse Dirt can now be crafted with 2 Dirt and 2 Gravel. Coarse Dirt can be tilled back into Dirt with a Hoe, making Dirt renewable</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">[Item Additions]</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Added Chain, allowing Chain Armor to be crafted</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Added Ender Tears, a rare drop from Enderman. Combine tears with items to repair them</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Hellfire Ingots, crafted from 4 Blaze Powder, 4 Iron and 1 Magma Cream. Creates very durable equipment</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Hellfire tools require Blaze Rods as a shaft</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Iron Arrows can now be crafted with Iron as a tip</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Fire Arrows can now be crafted with Fire Charges as a tip</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Raw Mutton now drops from Sheep&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Pumpkin Pie can now be crafted with 1 Pumpkin, 1 Sugar and 1 Egg</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Mushroom Stew and Potions now stack to 8</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Snowballs now stack to 64 and deal 5 damage(from 3) to Blaze. They now damage Magma Cubes as well</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">[Mob Changes]</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Added mob variants. Most mobs have a chance of having a different appearance&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Zombies now wear Chain Armor. They drop Chain and Cookies</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Zombies have a chance of wearing different armor types and dropping their respective material&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Skeletons have a 1 in 50 chance of being Helletons. Helletons have a 1 in 20 chance of dropping a special bow</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Blaze now naturally spawn in the Nether</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Magma Cubes are now 10 times more common(Which is still uncommon)</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Enderman now teleport randomly upon getting hit, they deal more damage, they're faster and teleport more erratically</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Squid now have a 1/100 chance of dropping a Fishing Rod(For Peaceful players)</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">[Fishing Improvements]</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Fishing now has its own loot tables of Fish, Trash and Treasure instead of only giving Raw Fish</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-You can now fish in the Nether&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Fishing now gives Points based on loot table</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">[Hardcore Thrist]</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Drinking unpurified water gives you the Thirst potion effect, disabling natural healing</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-The Thirst meter dropping below 30% disables natural healing&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Water Bottles can be purified in the Furnace</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Various Drinks can be made by combining a fruit/vegetable with Sugar and Purified Water(Pumpkins only require an Empty Bottle)</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-A Flask can be crafted with 6 Leather and 1 Iron. It can be filled from a water source and has 8 uses</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-A Flask can be purified with a Powder Filter, crafted with 3 Paper and 1 Gunpowder</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-You can drink Milk to restore Thirst</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">[Other]</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Furnaces now accept many new fuel sources</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Gold Tools are now a tier between Iron and Diamond. Gold is now required to mine Diamonds</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Explosions now drop all blocks/items, even Diamonds</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Iron Ore now generates up to y-128</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Mobs now have their own Score values. Score displays over the player&rsquo;s inventory</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Dropped items now render in 3D. All items render in 2D on fast graphics</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">-Readded the Beta Nightmare sleeping mechanic</span></p>
